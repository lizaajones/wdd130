Macaroni pasta

  • 1 box pasta
  • 1/3 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/3 cup favorite Italian dressing
  • Optional: cut up carrots and celery

Step one: Start by boiling water on the stove

Step two: Once done boiling, add the box of pasta and wait for about 10-15 minutes until pasta is cooked

Step three: Pour pot into strainer and put pasta in a bowl and let it cool for 10 minutes.

Step four: Add the mayonnaise and dressing. You can also add cut up vegetables of your choice. Stir together and you're done!

Green Smoothie

  • 1 cup of spinach
  • 1 cup of sliced banannas
  • 1 cup of peeled apples
  • 1/2cup of water
  • 3 iced cubes

Step one: Gather ingredients and put exact amounts into the blender

Step two: Once blended add ice cubes

Step three: Enjoy!

Protein Style Burger

  • 1 package of ground beef
  • Burger seasonings of your choice
  • Lettuce

Step one: Take out ground beef and roll into balls.

Step two: Flatten them out and place them on a hot pan to let them cook.

Step three: Flip the ground beef patty so the other side is cooked. Once done place on a plate.

Step four: Place lettuce down and put a patty on one then cover the patty with another piece of lettuce.

Step five: Then you are done! I had a side of corn with this, the best corn to get is Birds Eye Steamfresh Super Sweet Corn.

Taco Bowl

  • 1 pacakage of ground beef
  • 1 packet of taco seasonings of your choice
  • Lettuce
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 onion
  • Cilantro
  • 2 limes
  • Shredded cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Guacamole or Salsa or both!
  • Corn

Step one: Start by making your pico de gallo: cut up the tomato, onion, and cilantro and mix together. Once mixed, add a lime and dash of salt. There you have the best pico de gallo. Now set aside.

Step two: On a hot pan, place the ground beef on it and use a spatchula to break it up into crumbles. Add in the taco seasoning. Flip it around so it gets cooked all over.

Step three: Once done, place it aside. Start to cut up some lettuce.

Step four: Microwave a pack of Birds Eye Steamfresh Super Sweet Corn.

Step five: Get a bowl and combine everything to your liking! Add cheese, sour cream, lime, salsa if you want it's all yours.

Step six: Enjoy!
